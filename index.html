    THE SIGNATURES

    Asam Laksa

    A sour and spicy noodle soup in tamarind broth, topped with mackerel flakes, cucumber, pineapple, and mint for a bold, refreshing flavor.

    • Fishy Flavour
    • Fresh Herbs
    • Sour Kick
    Char Kuey Teow

    Flat rice noodles stir-fried over high heat with prawns, egg, bean sprouts, and chives, infused with the smoky aroma of “wok hei.”

    • Wok Hei
    • Savory Bite
    • Umami Burst
    Hokkien Mee

    Yellow noodles and vermicelli in a rich, red prawn broth, pork slices, hard-boiled egg, and a spoonful of spicy sambal for extra heat.

    • Spicy Flavour
    • Contain Seafood
    • Bold Aroma
